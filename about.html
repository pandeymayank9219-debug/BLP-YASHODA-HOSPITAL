About the Hospital

Healthcare built around dignity, trust, and clear medical guidance.

BLP YASHODA Hospital and Research Centre combines physician-led care, structured communication, diagnostics support, and accessible branch presence for families across Gonda.

What defines our care model

  • Patient-first communication with calm consultation support
  • Branch accessibility for wider community care access
  • Emergency coordination through the central helpline
  • Practical follow-up care for better treatment continuity
Our Mission

Deliver safe, ethical, and responsive healthcare for every patient family.

We believe hospital care should feel reliable, respectful, and easy to understand. That is why our team focuses on diagnosis clarity, specialist guidance, and practical treatment communication at every step.

Our Vision

Create a modern care environment where speed and empathy work together.

Our vision is to continue building a patient experience that combines trusted physicians, emergency readiness, community reach, and a cleaner digital-first consultation journey.
