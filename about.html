About Our Hospital

Healthcare built around dignity, trust, and modern patient experience.

BLP YASHODA Hospital and Research Centre combines physician-led care, emergency readiness, diagnostics support, and accessible branch service for families across Gonda district.

Our Mission

Deliver safe, ethical, and responsive healthcare with clear medical guidance.

We believe every patient deserves confidence in treatment decisions, easy access to consultation, and a hospital environment that feels organized, respectful, and reassuring.

Our Approach

Combine specialist consultation, branch support, and practical follow-up care.

Our hospital model focuses on smooth communication between doctors, staff, patients, and attendants so care remains understandable at every stage.
